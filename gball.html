About Golden BALL

he Golden Ball Award is one of the most prestigious honors in football, presented to the best player at each FIFA World Cup. Introduced in 1982 by France Football in collaboration with FIFA, the Golden Ball Award recognizes the tournament’s most outstanding performer, as voted by media representatives from a shortlist compiled by the FIFA Technical Study Group.
